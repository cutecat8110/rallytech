# RallyTech Selection Rubric

## Prefer

- industrial facilities
- control rooms
- instrumentation and sensors
- process plants
- site operations
- equipment close-ups with clear technical context
- wide shots that can support hero layouts
- cleaner detail shots for service cards or side sections

## Avoid

- generic handshake or meeting-room stock
- unrelated consumer lifestyle imagery
- obvious startup-office visuals
- heavy watermarking or branding
- visible competitor logos
- images that imply the wrong industry
- images that are too busy for text overlays

## Composition Notes

- Hero images need space for headline and CTA overlay.
- Service-card imagery should read quickly at smaller sizes.
- Favor lighting and color that can adapt to the brand system without fighting it.
