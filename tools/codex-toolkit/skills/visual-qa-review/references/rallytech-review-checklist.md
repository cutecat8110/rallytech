# RallyTech Review Checklist

## Hierarchy

- Is the H1 clearly dominant?
- Does the supporting sentence help instead of repeating the headline?
- Are sections easy to scan in the intended reading order?

## Layout and Spacing

- Do blocks feel intentionally grouped?
- Are gaps consistent across sections?
- Do cards, grids, and sidebars align cleanly?

## CTA Logic

- Is there one clear primary action?
- Are secondary actions visually subordinate?
- Are repeated CTAs still meaningful in context?

## Trust and Proof

- Does the page show capability, process, reliability, or contactability?
- Are trust signals specific enough for B2B readers?
- Is fluff replacing evidence?

## Motion and Interaction

- Do scroll reveals support comprehension?
- Are hover, focus, active, and open states visible and consistent?
- Do accordion and tab interactions feel deliberate?

## Responsive Behavior

- Does the hero still read well on mobile?
- Do navigation, cards, and forms remain clear at narrow widths?
- Does anything collapse into visual noise?
