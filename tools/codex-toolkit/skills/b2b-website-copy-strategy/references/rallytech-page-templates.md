# RallyTech Page Templates

## Home

- H1: clear industrial positioning, not generic marketing
- Subhead: what the company integrates, supports, or delivers
- Trust layer: controls, commissioning, plant data, field execution, or equivalent proof
- Services intro: one sentence that frames the service set as an integrated delivery capability
- CTA: one primary action, one optional secondary action

## About

- H1: trusted partner or equivalent credibility statement
- Intro: company role, delivery approach, collaboration model
- Capability section: grouped strengths, not random feature lists
- Process section: how the team works from alignment through handover

## Services Overview

- H1: service overview with integration emphasis
- Intro: why the service stack matters to industrial clients
- Cards: each card should have a short service name, a concrete outcome, and a CTA

## Service Page

- H1: service name
- Introduction: what the service covers and where it fits in project delivery
- Deliverables: what the team actually provides
- Value: why the client should care operationally or commercially
- Proof or CTA: short quote, summary, or clear next step

## Contact

- H1: clear invitation to talk
- Intro: what kinds of inquiries are welcome
- Form support: set response expectations and reduce friction
- Direct contact: show phone, email, and address without extra filler
