## Company and site type

- Company: 雷力科技 / Rally Technology
- Site type: 工業自動化、儀控、資料平台與系統整合能力導向的企業官網重構
- Primary goal: 清楚呈現服務能力、建立信任、提升高意圖聯絡轉換
- This is not a SaaS or PLG site
- No free trial, self-serve signup, paywall, upgrade flow, or in-app onboarding in current scope

## Audience

- 業主端工程或廠務窗口
- 自動化 / 儀控 / 電氣主管與工程師
- 專案管理、採購或系統整合決策者

## Conversion model

- Primary conversions: 聯絡諮詢、專案初步洽談、能力資格確認
- Primary CTA family: 聯絡我們、洽詢服務、討論專案需求
- Key trust signals: 服務深度、整合方案可行性、安全與品質承諾、回應效率、技術可信度

## Messaging guardrails

- Tone: 專業、清楚、可信賴、穩定，不走誇張或創業 SaaS 話術
- Traditional Chinese first, English maintained in parallel
- Keep technical terms when useful: `SCADA`、`HMI`、`PLC`、`DCS`、`PI System`、`Historian`、`I&E`
- Do not invent certifications, client names, project counts, case-study outcomes, or performance claims
- For copy decisions, prefer technical credibility, maintainability, commissioning readiness, traceability, and cross-team coordination over hype
