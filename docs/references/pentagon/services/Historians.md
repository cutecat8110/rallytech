## Historians

Historian 歷史資料系統

Historian data is pivotal in the seamless operation of oil and gas, specifically of RNG companies. It ensures operational efficiency, regulatory compliance, product quality, and informed decision-making. Moreover, it bolsters sustainability initiatives, facilitates resource optimization, and empowers these firms to advance towards a greener and more sustainable energy landscape.

Historian 歷史資料系統是油氣產業，特別是 RNG（可再生天然氣）業者穩定運轉的重要基礎。它有助於掌握營運效率、法規符合性、產品品質與決策依據，同時支撐永續管理與資源最佳化，協助企業朝更具韌性且可持續的能源發展方向前進。

Here at Rally Technology, we developed a double-redundant microhistorian solution that provides you with local redundant storage as well as a cloud-based solution that allows you to have access to your data from any place on Earth without even being connected to the remote site.

雷力科技開發了雙重備援的 Micro Historian 解決方案，結合本地端冗餘儲存與雲端架構，讓客戶即使未直接連線至遠端站點，也能從各地安全存取關鍵資料。
